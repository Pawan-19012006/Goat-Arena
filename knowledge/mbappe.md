# Origins
Kylian Mbappé was born in 1998 in Paris, France. He grew up in Bondy and trained at the INF Clairefontaine academy before Monaco signed him to their youth system.

# History
Mbappé made his professional Monaco debut in 2015 at age 16. In 2017, PSG signed him on loan with a €180m purchase option. After seven record-breaking seasons at PSG, he completed a free transfer to Real Madrid in 2024.

# Country
Mbappé represents the France National Team, serving as the team captain.

# Club Career
Monaco (2015–2017), Paris Saint-Germain (2017–2024), and Real Madrid (2024–present).

# Records
Mbappé is PSG's all-time leading scorer (256 goals). He is the youngest player to score a World Cup final hat-trick (2022) and the youngest to reach 40 UEFA Champions League goals.

# Achievements
Mbappé won the 2018 FIFA World Cup, the 2021 UEFA Nations League, 6 Ligue 1 titles, and has claimed multiple Ligue 1 Player of the Year awards.

# Managers
Mbappé played under Leonardo Jardim, Thomas Tuchel, Mauricio Pochettino, Didier Deschamps, and Carlo Ancelotti.

# Major Tournaments
Mbappé won the 2018 FIFA World Cup (winning Best Young Player) and finished runner-up in 2022, winning the Golden Boot with 8 goals.

# Recent Form
Mbappé finished as Ligue 1 top scorer in 2023-24 with PSG and led France to the semi-finals of Euro 2024 before starting his debut season with Real Madrid.

# Strengths
Explosive speed off the mark, rapid ball carry, clinical inside-the-box finishing, and elite 1v1 dribbling execution.

# Weaknesses
Extremely low defensive work rate and tracking back, and can be isolated when space in behind deep defensive blocks is congested.

# Debate Points
Mbappé has already dominated a World Cup at age 19 and has proven himself on the biggest matches, scoring a hat-trick in a World Cup final.

# Counter Points
Point out that he spent his entire peak playing in Ligue 1 (a lower-ranked league) and has yet to win a UEFA Champions League title or a Ballon d'Or.
