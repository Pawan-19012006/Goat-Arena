# KYLIAN MBAPPE
## HISTORY
Kylian Mbappé was born in 1998 in Paris, France. He developed at the Clairefontaine academy and began his professional career at Monaco, making his debut at age 16. In 2017, he signed for Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) for €180m, becoming the most expensive teenager in history. In 2024, he completed a high-profile transfer to Real Madrid. He is France's current captain and marquee talent.
## RECORDS
Mbappé is PSG's all-time leading goalscorer (over 250 goals). He is the youngest player to score in a World Cup final since Pelé (2018), and became only the second player in history to score a hat-trick in a FIFA World Cup final (2022).
## ACHIEVEMENTS
Mbappé won the 2018 FIFA World Cup with France and the 2021 UEFA Nations League. He was the runner-up in the 2022 FIFA World Cup, winning the Golden Boot (8 goals). He has claimed 6 Ligue 1 titles (1 with Monaco, 5 with PSG) and multiple Ligue 1 Player of the Year awards.
## TACTICS
Mbappé is a highly explosive forward who operates as a left winger or central striker. He relies on blistering pace, direct dribbling, acceleration off the mark, and lethal near-post finishing. He thrives in transition and counter-attack tactics under Didier Deschamps and Thomas Tuchel.
## WEAKNESSES
Mbappé shows low defensive tracking back and press participation. He can be isolated when space in behind is congested by deep defensive blocks.
