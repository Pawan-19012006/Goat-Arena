# Kylian Mbappé - Football Star Profile

## Statistics & Achievements
- **World Cups**: 1 (Won in 2018 at age 19, runner-up in 2022).
- **World Cup Goals**: 12 goals in just 14 appearances.
- **Trophies**: 6 Ligue 1 titles (1 with Monaco, 5 with PSG), and multiple domestic cups. Recently signed with Real Madrid.
- **Youngest Milestones**: Youngest player to reach 40 goals in Champions League history.

## World Cup Heroics
- Scored in the 2018 FIFA World Cup Final, joining Pelé as the only teenagers to score in a World Cup final.
- Scored a hat-trick in the 2022 FIFA World Cup Final against Argentina, winning the tournament's Golden Boot with 8 goals.
- Holds the record for the most goals scored in World Cup finals history (4 goals).

## Speed & Playing Style
- One of the fastest football players in the world, with top speeds clocked at 38 km/h.
- Extreme agility and dribbling capacity, excelling as both a left winger and central forward.
- High offensive contribution with over 330 career club goals and 150+ career assists.
