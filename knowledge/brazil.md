# Brazil National Team Profile

## World Cup & Copa América Legacy
- **World Cups**: 5 titles (1958, 1962, 1970, 1994, 2002) - The most in international football history.
- **Copa América**: 9 titles.
- **World Cup Consistency**: The only country to qualify and play in every single FIFA World Cup in history.
- **World Cup Goals**: All-time leading scorer in World Cup history with over 2,200 goals across all tournaments.

## Jogo Bonito & Global Talent
- Synonymous with "The Beautiful Game" (Jogo Bonito) characterized by flair, creative dribbling, attacking freedom, and technical brilliance.
- Home to Pelé, the only player to win 3 FIFA World Cups (1958, 1962, 1970) and score over 1,200 career goals.
- Produced the most Ballon d'Or winners from South America, including Ronaldo Nazário, Ronaldinho, Rivaldo, Kaká, and Romário.
- Exported thousands of professional football players globally, establishing Brazil as the cultural capital of football.
