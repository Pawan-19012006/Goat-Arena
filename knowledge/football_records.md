# General Football Records & Statistics

## All-Time Goalscoring Records (Official Match Tallies)
1. **Cristiano Ronaldo**: 900+ official goals (Real Madrid, Sporting Lisbon, Manchester United, Juventus, Al Nassr, Portugal).
2. **Lionel Messi**: 850+ official goals (Barcelona, PSG, Inter Miami, Argentina).
3. **Pelé**: 762 official goals (Santos, New York Cosmos, Brazil). Note: Claimed 1,281 goals including unofficial friendlies.
4. **Romário**: 755 official goals (Vasco da Gama, PSV, Barcelona, Brazil).
5. **Ferenc Puskás**: 724 official goals (Budapest Honvéd, Real Madrid, Hungary).

## Assist Records (All-Time Modern Football Era)
1. **Lionel Messi**: 380+ official assists for club and country.
2. **Thomas Müller**: 260+ official assists (Bayern Munich, Germany).
3. **Luis Suárez**: 250+ official assists (Ajax, Liverpool, Barcelona, Uruguay).
4. **Neymar**: 240+ official assists (Santos, Barcelona, PSG, Brazil).
5. **Kevin De Bruyne**: 240+ official assists (Chelsea, Wolfsburg, Manchester City, Belgium).

## Calendar Year & Season Peaks
- **Most Goals in a Calendar Year**: Lionel Messi (91 goals in 2012).
- **Most Goals in a European Club Season**: Lionel Messi (73 goals in 2011/12).
- **Most Assists in a Club Season**: Lionel Messi and Thomas Müller share records with 21 league assists in a single season.
- **Most Goals in UEFA Champions League Season**: Cristiano Ronaldo (17 goals in 2013/14).
