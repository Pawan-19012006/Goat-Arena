# Cristiano Ronaldo - Football Legend Profile

## Statistics & Achievements
- **Ballon d'Ors**: 5 (Won in 2008, 2013, 2014, 2016, 2017).
- **Career Goals**: Over 900 official career goals, making him the all-time leading goalscorer in professional football history.
- **Trophies**: 35 major trophies, including 5 UEFA Champions Leagues (1 with Manchester United, 4 with Real Madrid), Euro 2016 and Nations League with Portugal, and league titles in England, Spain, and Italy.
- **International Goals**: Over 130 goals for Portugal, the all-time international scoring record.

## Champions League Dominance (Mr. Champions League)
- All-time top scorer in UEFA Champions League history with 140 goals.
- All-time top assist provider in Champions League history with 42 assists.
- Most goals scored in a single Champions League campaign (17 goals in 2013/14).
- Most Champions League knockout stage goals in history (67 goals), proving unparalleled clutch performance in big games.
- The only player to score in 3 separate Champions League finals.

## Longevity & Athleticism
- Scored over 400 goals since turning 30 years old, showcase of work ethic and training regime.
- Has won the league golden boot in 4 different leagues: Premier League (England), La Liga (Spain), Serie A (Italy), and Saudi Pro League (Saudi Arabia).
- Renowned for aerial capability, vertical leap, weak-foot scoring, and speed.
