# CRISTIANO RONALDO
## HISTORY
Cristiano Ronaldo was born in 1985 in Madeira, Portugal. He started his career at Sporting CP before being signed by Sir Alex Ferguson at Manchester United in 2003. In 2009, he moved to Real Madrid for a world-record fee. After successful spells at Juventus (2018-2021) and a return to Manchester United, he joined Saudi Pro League club Al-Nassr in 2023. He is Portugal's greatest legend alongside Eusebio and Luis Figo.
## RECORDS
Ronaldo is the all-time leading goalscorer in international football history (over 130 goals). He is the all-time top scorer in the UEFA Champions League (140 goals) and holds the record for most goals in a single UCL season (17 goals in 2013-14). He also holds the record for scoring in 5 consecutive FIFA World Cups.
## ACHIEVEMENTS
Ronaldo has won 5 Ballon d'Or awards (2008, 2013, 2014, 2016, 2017). He won UEFA Euro 2016 and the 2019 UEFA Nations League with Portugal. He has claimed 5 UEFA Champions League titles (1 with Manchester United, 4 with Real Madrid) and league championships in England, Spain, and Italy.
## TACTICS
Ronaldo transitioned from a dribbling winger to a lethal, central target man. He excels in aerial duels, penalty box movement, off-ball positioning, and direct free kicks. He played his peak years under Jose Mourinho, Carlo Ancelotti, and Zinedine Zidane.
## WEAKNESSES
Ronaldo has minimal involvement in defensive pressing and build-up play. He relies on wings or midfielders to deliver crosses into the box, and has shown a drop in completed dribbles per game in recent seasons.
