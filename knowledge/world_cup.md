# FIFA World Cup History & Statistics

## Tournament Summary
- Founded in 1930, played every 4 years (except 1942 and 1946 due to World War II).
- The ultimate trophy in international football.

## Top Winning Nations
- **Brazil**: 5 titles (1958, 1962, 1970, 1994, 2002)
- **Germany**: 4 titles (1954, 1974, 1990, 2014)
- **Italy**: 4 titles (1934, 1938, 1982, 2006)
- **Argentina**: 3 titles (1978, 1986, 2022)
- **France**: 2 titles (1998, 2018)
- **Uruguay**: 2 titles (1930, 1950)
- **England**: 1 title (1966)
- **Spain**: 1 title (2010)

## Top Individual Performers
- **Pelé**: Only player to win 3 World Cups (1958, 1962, 1970).
- **Miroslav Klose**: All-time leading World Cup goalscorer with 16 goals (Germany).
- **Ronaldo Nazário**: Second all-time top scorer with 15 goals (Brazil).
- **Lionel Messi**: Most appearances in World Cup history (26 matches) and first to win two Golden Balls (2014, 2022).
- **Kylian Mbappé**: Most goals scored in World Cup Finals (4 goals), including a hat-trick in the 2022 Final.
- **Just Fontaine**: Most goals scored in a single World Cup tournament (13 goals in 1958, France).
