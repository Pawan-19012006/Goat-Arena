# UEFA Champions League History & Statistics

## Tournament Overview
- Originally known as the European Cup, rebranded as the Champions League in 1992.
- The premier club tournament in European and global football.

## Top Winning Clubs
- **Real Madrid**: 15 titles (Most in history, including 5 consecutive from 1956-1960, and 3 consecutive from 2016-2018).
- **AC Milan**: 7 titles.
- **Bayern Munich**: 6 titles.
- **Liverpool**: 6 titles.
- **Barcelona**: 5 titles (1992, 2006, 2009, 2011, 2015).
- **Ajax**: 4 titles.
- **Manchester United**: 3 titles.
- **Inter Milan**: 3 titles.

## Individual Player Records
- **Most Titles**: Paco Gento, Dani Carvajal, Luka Modrić, Toni Kroos, Nacho, and Cristiano Ronaldo all hold the record with 6 Champions League titles.
- **Top Goalscorers**:
  1. **Cristiano Ronaldo**: 140 goals in 183 appearances (Real Madrid, Manchester United, Juventus).
  2. **Lionel Messi**: 129 goals in 163 appearances (Barcelona, PSG).
  3. **Robert Lewandowski**: 94 goals (Dortmund, Bayern, Barcelona).
  4. **Karim Benzema**: 90 goals (Lyon, Real Madrid).
- **Top Assist Providers**:
  1. **Cristiano Ronaldo**: 42 assists.
  2. **Lionel Messi**: 40 assists.
  3. **Ángel Di María**: 38 assists.
  4. **Neymar**: 33 assists.
- **Single Season Record**: Cristiano Ronaldo scored 17 goals during the 2013/14 campaign.
- **Knockout Stage Record**: Cristiano Ronaldo is the all-time leader in knockout stage goals with 67 goals.
