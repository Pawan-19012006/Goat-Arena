# Argentina National Team Profile

## World Cup & Copa América Legacy
- **World Cups**: 3 titles (1978, 1986 under Diego Maradona, 2022 under Lionel Messi).
- **Copa América**: 16 titles (All-time record holder, including back-to-back wins in 2021 and 2024).
- **Finalissima**: Winners in 2022, defeating UEFA Champions Italy 3-0.
- **FIFA Ranking**: 1st in the world (post-2022).

## Tactical Core (Scaloneta)
- Managed by Lionel Scaloni since 2018. Developed a balanced team structure focusing on high-workrate midfields (De Paul, Mac Allister, Enzo Fernández) and solid defense (Emiliano Martínez, Cristian Romero).
- Achieved a historic 36-match unbeaten run between 2019 and 2022, the second-longest in international football history.
- Known for mental resilience under pressure, winning multiple penalty shootouts in major tournaments.
