# Erling Haaland - Football Star Profile

## Statistics & Achievements
- **Premier League Records**: Broke the all-time scoring record in a single 38-game Premier League season with 36 goals in 2022/23.
- **Trophies**: Historic Treble with Manchester City in 2022/23 (UEFA Champions League, Premier League, FA Cup).
- **Goal Ratio**: Averaged 0.95 goals per game across his professional career at Dortmund and Manchester City.
- **Champions League Milestones**: Fastest and youngest player to reach 40 Champions League goals (doing so in just 35 matches).

## Physicality & Goalscoring Efficiency
- Towering physical height (6'4") combined with explosive acceleration (top speed of 36 km/h) and aerial strength.
- Renowned for off-the-ball movement and space creation.
- Highly efficient in front of goal, regularly outperforming his Expected Goals (xG) metrics and requiring minimal touches to decide matches.
