# ERLING HAALAND
## HISTORY
Erling Haaland was born in 2000 in Leeds, England, to Norwegian footballer Alfie Haaland. He started at Bryne and Molde in Norway under Ole Gunnar Solskjaer. He joined Red Bull Salzburg in 2019, Dortmund in 2020, and transferred to Manchester City in 2022. He is Norway's superstar forward alongside Martin Ødegaard.
## RECORDS
Haaland holds the record for most goals in a single Premier League season (36 goals in 35 appearances during 2022-23). He is the fastest player in history to score 30 Champions League goals (25 games) and the youngest to reach 40 goals.
## ACHIEVEMENTS
Haaland won the historic continental treble (Premier League, FA Cup, UEFA Champions League) with Manchester City in 2022-23. He claimed the Premier League Golden Boot in 2023 and 2024, won the DFB-Pokal with Dortmund in 2021, and won the Austrian Bundesliga with Salzburg.
## TACTICS
Haaland is a physical, target striker who operates through central space. He uses massive physical strength, explosive sprints, elite off-ball movement, and one-touch finishing. He excels in direct transitions and Pep Guardiola's positional attack systems.
## WEAKNESSES
Haaland has low involvement in build-up play and passing combinations, and can have low touch counts when closely marked by deep blocks.
